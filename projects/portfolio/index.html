I am Steve Rogers

Steve Rogers, also known as Captain America, is a fictional superhero character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Joe Simon and artist Jack Kirby, the character first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941. Steve Rogers is a patriotic super-soldier who fought in World War II and was enhanced to the peak of human physicality by the Super-Soldier Serum. He is known for his leadership, courage, and unwavering sense of justice. Captain America is a founding member of the Avengers and has been portrayed in various movies and TV shows.
